About Us

Welcome to Radhakrishnan Bhawan,

One of the most prestigious and well-equipped hostels in IIT Roorkee, named after the renowned Indian philosopher and statesman Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan. Our hostel is designed to provide a comfortable and peaceful living experience, well-furnished rooms that offer all the necessary amenities. We take pride in our sports club, which offers facilities like pool tables, table tennis, and indoor games to help students unwind after a long day of classes. In addition, we offer a 24-hour Air-Conditioned study room with high-speed internet connectivity, providing students with a comfortable and conducive environment to work.
We understand that good food is crucial to a student's well-being, and our mess serves nutritious and delicious food to cater to all tastes and dietary requirements. Our hostel is located in the heart of the IIT Roorkee campus, with lush green gardens that offer a serene environment for our residents to relax and unwind. We take pride in maintaining a clean and well-maintained hostel, with dedicated staff available to address any issues or concerns that our residents may have.

We look forward to welcoming you to Radhakrishnan Bhawan and hope that your stay with us is comfortable, enjoyable, and memorable.
